BIRDTREE

Branding
Brand Name Branding and Logo Development | Brand Philosophy | POP & Marketing Collaterals | Packaging and Product Design | Offline and Digital Identity
This project was a holistic branding journey. The client, previously focused on wholesale dry-fruits, aimed to make a strong foray into the retail market—beginning with the creation of a compelling brand identity. We developed the name BirdTree, symbolizing a harmonious blend of aspiration and rooted authenticity: a brand that soars with health-forward intent while remaining grounded in quality and value. Vibrant, retail-oriented packaging was meticulously crafted—from design and printing to the end-to-end supply of materials—ensuring a seamless, high-quality rollout that positioned the brand for a premium market debut.
