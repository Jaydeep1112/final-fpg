MANAMA

Branding
Branding & Brand Consulting | Ecom Website | Packaging Advertisement Marketing | Print Production Supplies
Collaborating with Manama has always been creatively rewarding—though intricate by nature. Each packaging project begins well in advance, much like painting on a blank canvas. The client’s openness to experimentation perfectly aligns with their tagline... Always Something New! this also empowers our design team to continually deliver fresh, distinctive concepts. Over the years, we’ve contributed consistently across all touchpoints: creative labels for each SKU, distinct hoardings, banners, and marketing collateral, along with the development of their e-commerce website and SEO strategies to drive online sales. Our involvement goes beyond design—we’ve also provided end-to-end print production support, ensuring that printed labels mirror the vibrancy and precision as visualised. From high-quality brochures to impactful exhibition booths, every element has been crafted to reflect Manama’s detail to quality.
