GROWN DIAMOND

Brand Name Branding and Logo Development | Brand Philosophy | POP & Marketing Collaterals | Website
Grown Diamond Corporation approached us in 2012 to help shape their identity around the emerging concept of lab-grown diamonds. We created a distinctive brand logo that reflected innovation and elegance, laying the foundation for broader market awareness. Our partnership extended into designing exhibition stalls, brochures, a dedicated website, and a full suite of sales and marketing materials—each crafted to educate, inspire, and elevate the brand. Over the years, we’ve continued to evolve these assets annually, ensuring the brand stays relevant, refined, and resonant in a growing market
