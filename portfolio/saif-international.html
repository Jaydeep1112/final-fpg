SAIF INTERNATIONAL

Branding and Logo Development | Brand Consulting | Advertisement Material | Print Supplies | Website | Photo & Video work
Our collaboration with Saif International remains a defining chapter in our journey, marking one of our earliest international assignments back in 2005. We began by creating a bold and memorable logo for their brand HABBA HABBA, which later led to a complete upgrade of the parent company’s identity—including its logo, website, profile, and brochures. We also developed wholesale packaging solutions tailored for their export clientele, reinforcing brand consistency across bulk produce.
In 2025, as Saif International commemorated its 50th anniversary, we had the privilege of leading their branding evolution once again—designing a refreshed website, an updated company brochure, and a cinematic profile documentary that honored their legacy while presenting a dynamic, future-forward vision.
